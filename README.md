Android developer
-----------------

Android development is my passion and I have been pursuing it since I started my software engineering degree at Ahram Canadian University (ACU). I have a strong foundation in software engineering principles and programming languages, especially Java and Kotlin, which I have used to create innovative mobile apps for various academic and personal projects. I recently completed a four-month Android Developer Trainee program at the Information Technology Institute (ITI), where I gained hands-on experience in developing and testing Android applications using REST APIs, JSON, and dependency injection. I also learned how to use Android Studio, Firebase, and Git to manage and deploy my projects. I am now looking for internship or entry-level opportunities in Android development where I can apply my skills and knowledge to solve real-world problems and contribute to cutting-edge projects. I am eager to learn from experienced developers and mentors, and to grow as a professional and as a team player.
